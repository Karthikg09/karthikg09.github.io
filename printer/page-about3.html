About Us

About the Company

Suphala Farmer Producer Company is located in Nitte, in Udupi district. It is primarily a Jackfruit Processing Cluster which aims at empowering small and marginal farmers, rural women, farm labourers and unemployed youth. It has established a model partnership to collaborate with the farmers and unemployed youth and support them by providing quality seeds and saplings, educate them on the benefits of cultivating Jackfruit and provide technical support to improve the product quality.Suphala not only aims at assuring consistent earnings to farmers by assured buy back schemes but will also provide employment and growth opportunities to local talent.
